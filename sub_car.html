Types of Cars

Sedan

Sedans are known for their comfortable ride, fuel efficiency, and ample trunk space, making them great for families and long commutes.

SUV

SUVs offer a higher seating position, more cargo space, and are often equipped with all-wheel drive for off-road capabilities.

Convertible

Convertibles provide an open-air driving experience with the option to retract the roof, combining style and performance.

Truck

Trucks are designed for hauling and towing heavy loads, featuring powerful engines and durable construction for rugged use.
